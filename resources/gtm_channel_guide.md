# GTM Channel Guide

A deep-dive reference on every channel type in the GTM Canvas framework. Use this when you or your facilitator need more context on a specific channel before committing to it.

---

## Quadrant 1 — Inbound Organic (Pull, Free)

These channels bring users to you without paid spend. They compound over time but take longer to show results.

| Channel | Best For | Time to Results | Key Metric |
|---------|----------|-----------------|------------|
| SEO / Blog | B2B SaaS, tools, marketplaces | 3–12 months | Organic sessions, keyword rankings |
| YouTube | Tutorial-heavy products, developer tools | 2–6 months | Views, subscribers, CTR to landing page |
| Reddit / Quora | Niche communities, technical products | Immediate–weeks | Upvotes, referral traffic |
| Twitter/X content | Founders, B2B, creator tools | Weeks–months | Impressions, profile clicks |
| LinkedIn content | B2B, enterprise, professional services | Weeks–months | Post reach, connection requests |
| Product Led Growth (PLG) | SaaS with a freemium model | Ongoing | Activation rate, viral coefficient |
| Facebook Groups | Consumer products, local audiences | Weeks | Engagement, group members |
| Free tools / calculators | SEO + brand awareness | 3–6 months | Tool usage, backlinks earned |
| Hashtag campaigns | B2C, brand building | Days–weeks | Impressions, UGC volume |
| Community participation | Product Hunt, Indie Hackers, HN | Immediate | Upvotes, comments, referrals |

**When to choose Inbound Organic:**
- You have a content-friendly angle (education, opinion, tutorials)
- You have time and discipline to publish consistently
- You want a compounding, long-term traffic engine

## Quadrant 2 — Inbound Paid (Pull, Paid)

Immediate traffic with spend. Requires budget and iterative testing.

| Channel | Best For | Min. Viable Budget | Key Metric |
|---------|----------|-------------------|------------|
| Google Search Ads | High-intent buyers, B2B | $500–$2K/mo | CPC, conversion rate, CAC |
| Meta / Instagram Ads | B2C, visual products, consumer apps | $300–$1K/mo | CPM, CTR, ROAS |
| LinkedIn Ads | B2B, enterprise, HR/recruiting | $2K+/mo | CPL, MQL rate |
| Twitter/X Ads | Tech-savvy audiences, B2B | $500–$1K/mo | Engagement rate, CTR |
| Influencer campaigns | Consumer products, lifestyle, creator tools | Varies | Reach, promo code usage |
| Directory submissions | SaaS (G2, Capterra, Product Hunt) | Free–$500/mo | Listing clicks, review velocity |
| Banner / Display Ads | Brand awareness, retargeting | $500/mo+ | Impressions, retargeting CVR |

**When to choose Inbound Paid:**
- You need fast signal on what messaging works
- You have validated product-market fit and want to scale
- Your CAC economics are clearly understood

## Quadrant 3 — Outbound Organic (Push, Free)

You go to your users instead of waiting for them to come to you. High effort, high control.

| Channel | Best For | Time Investment | Key Metric |
|---------|----------|-----------------|------------|
| Cold email | B2B, SaaS, marketplaces | High | Reply rate (>5% = good), meeting rate |
| LinkedIn outreach | B2B, professional services | High | Acceptance rate, reply rate |
| Event / meetup networking | All stages, community-led | Medium | Conversations, follow-ups |
| Webinars / info sessions | Education-heavy products | High | Attendees, post-webinar conversion |
| Guest podcasting | Thought leadership, B2B | Medium | Downloads, listener conversion |
| PR / press outreach | Launch moments, funding news | High | Coverage placements, DA of publications |
| Discord / Slack communities | Developer tools, crypto, niche SaaS | Ongoing | Replies, DMs, community trust score |
| Reddit engagement | Niche products, developer tools | Medium | Upvotes, DM requests |

**When to choose Outbound Organic:**
- You're pre-product-market fit and need direct user feedback loops
- You have a small, well-defined ICP (ideal customer profile)
- You want full-cycle control over the sales motion

## Quadrant 4 — Outbound Paid (Push, Paid)

Mass reach with budget. Better for consumer products and brand awareness phases.

| Channel | Best For | Budget Range | Key Metric |
|---------|----------|-------------|------------|
| Newsletter sponsorships | B2B, creator tools, productivity | $200–$5K/issue | Open rate, click-through, code redemptions |
| Podcast sponsorships | B2B, consumer products | $500–$10K/episode | Download reach, promo code usage |
| Giveaways / contests | B2C, consumer apps, growth hacks | $200–$2K | Participants, email captures, sharing rate |
| Coupon codes | E-commerce, marketplaces | Cost of discount | Redemption rate, LTV of promo users |
| Event / conference sponsorships | Enterprise B2B, niche verticals | $2K–$50K | Business cards, demo bookings |
| TV / Radio / Print | Late-stage, mass market | $10K+ | Brand lift surveys, spot recall |
| Transport advertising | Local businesses, consumer | $500–$5K/mo | Impressions (estimated) |

**When to choose Outbound Paid:**
- You're post-PMF and scaling brand awareness
- You want to reach audiences that don't self-select through search
- You have a strong, compelling offer to attach to the spend

## Partnerships

Partnerships multiply reach without proportional cost. The best ones are win-win distributions.

| Type | Example | What You Get | What They Get |
|------|---------|-------------|---------------|
| Brand co-marketing | Co-create content with a complementary brand | Their audience | Your audience |
| Platform integration | Build a Notion/Slack/Linear plugin | In-app distribution | Ecosystem value |
| Community partnership | Partner with a Slack group or Discord | Access to engaged members | Expert content / value |
| Distributor/reseller | White-label or referral arrangement | Sales without headcount | Revenue share |
| Event co-sponsorship | Joint booth or session at a conference | Shared cost, shared leads | Same |
| NGO / Government | Subsidized access program | Press coverage, credibility | Your product's impact |

**Partnership evaluation checklist:**
- [ ] Do their users match our ICP?
- [ ] Is the relationship reciprocal (mutual value)?
- [ ] What's the minimum viable commitment to test it?
- [ ] Can we measure results clearly?

## Channel Selection Heuristics

Use this decision table to pick your first 2–3 channels:

| Signal | Recommended Channel Mix |
|--------|------------------------|
| Niche B2B, small ICP | Outbound Organic + LinkedIn + Partnerships |
| Mass consumer product | Inbound Paid (Meta) + Inbound Organic (SEO/UGC) + Partnerships |
| Developer tool | Inbound Organic (Reddit/HN/GitHub) + Outbound Organic (Discord) |
| Marketplace | PLG + SEO + Outbound Organic to both sides |
| Local / geo-specific | Outbound Organic (events) + Outbound Paid (local ads) |
| Founder-led brand | Twitter/X + LinkedIn + Guest podcasting + PR |

> **Rule of thumb:** Start with 2 channels maximum. Get signal, iterate, then add a third.
