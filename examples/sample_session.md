# Sample GTM Canvas Session

This file illustrates how a complete facilitation session looks from start to finish. Use this as a reference when integrating the skill or to set user expectations.

---

## Setup

**Facilitator:**
> Welcome! I'm your GTM Canvas facilitator — let's build your go-to-market strategy together. Before we dive in, a few quick questions:
>
> 1. What's the name of your project?
> 2. Who's owning GTM execution?
> 3. What are you launching — in one sentence?

**User:**
> Project: InboxZero AI. GTM lead is me, Aisha. We're launching an AI email assistant that reads your inbox and drafts replies in your tone.

---

## Component 1 — Primary Feature

**Facilitator:**
> Love it. What is THE one feature users come to InboxZero AI for? The thing they'd be devastated to lose?

**User:**
> The auto-draft feature — it reads context and writes a reply in my writing style.

**Facilitator:**
> Your **Primary Feature = AI auto-drafting that matches the user's tone.** Strong, un-ignorable hook. ✓

---

## Component 2 — Primary Sellable Feature

**Facilitator:**
> What do customers actually pay for — same feature or different?

**User:**
> A Pro plan with unlimited drafts and multi-account support.

**Facilitator:**
> **Primary Sellable Feature = Unlimited drafts + multi-account management (Pro Plan).** Classic freemium shape — hook is free, monetisation is Pro. ✓

---

## Component 3 — Differentiator

**Facilitator:**
> What's your edge vs. Superhuman or a ChatGPT plugin?

**User:**
> Most tools give generic replies. Ours actually learns your tone from past emails — it sounds like *you*.

**Facilitator:**
> That's a sharp differentiator. **Differentiator = Tone-matching AI trained on the user's own email history.** ✓

---

## Component 4 — Alpha (Unfair Advantage)

**Facilitator:**
> What do you have that a well-funded competitor couldn't replicate in 6 months?

**User:**
> Our founder has 10 years of NLP research background and we have a proprietary fine-tuning pipeline.

**Facilitator:**
> Real moat. **Alpha = Proprietary NLP fine-tuning pipeline + deep NLP research expertise.** Takes years to replicate. ✓

---

## Component 5 — Handpick Users

**Facilitator:**
> Forget "target audience" — who are the first 100 actual humans you can text or DM right now?

**User:**
> My LinkedIn network of startup founders and VCs, ex-Google colleagues, Product Hunt communities.

**Facilitator:**
> Perfect pool. Quick table:

| Name | Email / Contact | Connection |
|------|-----------------|------------|
| James (ex-Google PM) | james@— | Close |
| Priya (YC founder) | @priya_yc | Medium |

> Who in that list would write about you without being asked?

**User:**
> Priya — she tweets about founder tools weekly, 8K followers.

**Facilitator:**
> **Influencer = Priya.** Onboard her first as a design partner. She'll write about you organically. ✓

---

## Component 6 — Profiling

**Facilitator:**
> Based on those 100 people, what do 2–3 typical user profiles look like?

**User:**
> 1. Solo founders with 100+ emails/day and no EA.
> 2. VCs responding to hundreds of portfolio founders.
> 3. Agency PMs juggling multiple client threads.

**Facilitator:**
```
Profile 1: "The Scrappy Founder"
  Age 25–40 | Goal: ship faster without drowning in email
  Pain: loses 2h/day to inbox | Online: Twitter/X, HN, Indie Hackers

Profile 2: "The Active VC"
  Age 30–50 | Goal: stay responsive without burning out
  Pain: hundreds of cold inbounds weekly | Online: LinkedIn, AngelList

Profile 3: "The Agency PM"
  Age 28–38 | Goal: keep multi-client threads organised
  Pain: context-switching chaos | Online: LinkedIn, Slack communities
```

---

## Component 7 — Source

**Facilitator:**
> Where do these profiles actually spend their time?

**User:**
> Hacker News, Product Hunt, Twitter/X, founder Slack groups. Also SaaStr.

**Facilitator:**
> **Online:** HN, Product Hunt, Twitter/X, Indie Hackers, YC Slack.  
> **Offline:** SaaStr, local founder meetups. ✓

---

## Component 8 — Channels

| Quadrant | Selected | First Action |
|----------|----------|-------------|
| Inbound Organic | Twitter/X, Product Hunt, HN Show HN | Post "How we built tone-matching AI" thread |
| Inbound Paid | Google Ads (email productivity keywords) | $500 test campaign |
| Outbound Organic | LinkedIn DMs, cold email to VC list | 50 personalised DMs this week |
| Outbound Paid | Founder newsletter sponsorship | Negotiate one-off slot |
| Partnerships | Product Hunt Ship + productivity tool co-launch | Reach out to Notion template creators |
